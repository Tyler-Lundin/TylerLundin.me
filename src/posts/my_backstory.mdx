# Hello, I am Tyler!

### I am a Web Developer

Becoming a Web Developer has been a long and difficult road of constant and endless failures inside of trial and error.
I started the Self-taught journey near the end of 2021, spending all my free time __without__ my friends to commit to something bigger in life.
Growing up I lacked discipline, my parents were divorced so I was given free reign over my life at a young age.  To say the least, I was
an awful student.  I never attempted to continue my education with college as I never felt a solid connection with those around me.

I have always felt a disconnect to a majority of people in life, wondering why I am different from the rest of the world but I have started to see
it in a different light over the last few years.  None of us know who or what we are, and we are all in the same ocean, in different boats.  Life 
is going to be full of amazing and terrible incidents and surprises, but it is on me to handle them all the same.  I value my integrity and honesty 
more then anything else I have.  I do good as much as I can, and I get back up when bad happens.  A huge positive on my life has been taking in different
philisophical ideas, a large one being stoicism.  In 2021, a girl who I had grown a deeper connection to then anyone else in my life had taken her life.  
I isolated away from everyone and kept my head down when I returned to work.  I took my frustrations out on weights inside of the gym day after day, week after week,
and up to now, year after year.  Life is much more precious to me now that I have experienced a major loss.  I don't want to take anything or anyone for granted, and 
I plan on living a life that I find fulfilling.

I will continue to learn and struggle every day for the good of myself and those around me.  I will continue to be a good person and help those who need it.  Life will knock
me down again and again but I will not lie down until my final day.  I love you all, thank you for reading.
